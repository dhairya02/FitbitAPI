# Privacy Policy for Fitbit Single-User Data Ingestion App

## Overview
Fitbit Single-User Data Ingestion App (the "Application") ingests Fitbit data to support research, analytics, and quality improvement efforts. We respect your privacy and describe below how information is collected, used, and protected.

## Information We Collect
The Application collects Fitbit data according to the scopes you authorize, which may include:
- Fitbit account identifier (user ID)
- Activity data (steps, distance, calories, active minutes, and related measures)
- Heart rate data, including intraday measurements if enabled
- Sleep data and related metrics when permission is granted
- Basic profile metadata exposed by Fitbit (such as age range, gender, locale, or time zone)
- Other Fitbit metrics covered by the authorized scopes

## How We Use Your Information
Collected information may be used to:
- Perform research analyses, data science, and quality-improvement studies
- Generate aggregated or de-identified reports and publications
- Improve algorithms, analytic pipelines, and related tooling for the study

## How We Store and Protect Your Information
- Data is stored within controlled environments such as secure servers, institutional storage, or encrypted databases.
- Reasonable technical and organizational measures (access controls, authentication, monitoring) are implemented to help protect the data.
- Access is limited to authorized members of the research or engineering team who require it for study operations.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure
- We do not sell or rent personal data.
- De-identified or aggregated findings may be shared with collaborators, funders, or in publications and presentations.
- Identifiable Fitbit data is not shared with third parties outside the research or operational team unless required by law, regulation, or institutional policy.

## Your Choices and Rights
- You may revoke the Application's access to your Fitbit data at any time through your Fitbit account settings.
- You may request that your identifiable data be deleted from the system, subject to applicable legal, regulatory, or institutional review board obligations.
- Participation is voluntary, and you may stop using the Application at any time.

## Data Retention
Data is retained for as long as necessary to conduct research analyses, fulfill quality-improvement objectives, and meet compliance requirements. De-identified or aggregated data may be retained for longer-term study or archival purposes.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The most current version will always be available at this URL, and continued use of the Application signifies acceptance of any updates.
